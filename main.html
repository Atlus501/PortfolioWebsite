Nonogram

This is the one I might have to explain because it is an extremely niche game. Essentially, a nonogram is where there are numbers on the sides of the board that serves as hints to tell you how to fill out the board. The way that you win is to fill in the board correctly by using the hints at the side. There are three different difficulties and the boards are completely randomized. (In retrospect, I may have overtuned the difficulty). This project helped me learn C# and share a difficult game that I think will really challenge players.
